About HANDS OF FIRE MINISTRIES TRUST.

OUR MISSION

HANDS OF FIRE MINISTRIES AND TRUST is a Non-Profit Charitable Organisation Based In Kanyakumari Whose Focus Is To Construct HOME For Homeless, Needy, Aged And Infirm, Forlorn, Isolated People And Unprotected Women Of The Society And Provide Them Welfare And Rehabilitation Facilities In A Home Like Atmosphere HANDS OF FIRE MINISTRIES AND TRUST is a non-profit charitable organisation whose also focus is the development and empowerment of women and girls in Tamilnadu..

We work with women and children from all communities in Kanyakumari who experience discrimination on a daily basis because of their low status in class, caste and gender. These women are greatly suppressed due to the patriarchal nature of their society and have little or no access to education. They are denied the right to choose their husband and often face a high level of verbal, physical and sexual abuse within their family circle and community. This is why HANDS OF FIRE MINISTRIES AND TRUST Trust was born in 2008: to empower these deprived women. And big vision To Construct HOME for Homeless people.

AIM

1. To help needy aged ( any person of either sex above 60 years), irrespective of caste or creed
2. For the above purpose and or in pursuance thereof to undertake, execute, support and assist any programme(s) or project(s) with no profit motive designed, to provide – Food, shelter and clothing for needy aged
3. Educational & recreational facilities to the aged and provide facilities for them to lead a religious & meditative life
4. To promote National Integration and Unity of the Country
5. To promote self confidence and spirit of self reliance among the Ashramites to enable them to acquire necessary skills and guide them to achieve excellence in their fields of activity.
6. To establish Model School providing education upto 10th Class to the children of the poor and economically backward communities around the area, including training in other crafts, enabling them to earn their livelihood after they leave the school and prove to be better citizens
7. To do all such things as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of the objectives or any of them.

OBJECTIVE

1. To provide comprehensive and quality care and to ensure that the resident homeless citizens live with respect and dignity in a secure, compassionate, congenial environment and surroundings.
2. To act as a centre where the knowledge and experiences of life of the senior citizens are utilized in a constructive manner so as to contribute more meaningfully to both, the residents and the society.
3. To establish a social institution where society not only interacts and looks after senior citizens but also learns from them “the much needed values of a healthy and peaceful society”.
4. To provide companionship, emotional support, therapeutic occupation, recreation facilities and activities to overcome social isolation.
5. To provide independence to senior citizens in daily life, and to re-establish high values of life nurtured by them.

Our Vision

Our vision is to construct HOME for Homeless, needy, aged and infirm, forlorn, isolated people and unprotected women of the society and provide them welfare and rehabilitation facilities in a home like atmosphere.
To provide home like environments and comfort to the old and deserving ladies.
To provide free of cost medical facilities
To create a community where the old, needy, forlorn, isolated and unprotected women of the society live together, share their feelings and console each other to develop a family in a home like atmosphere
To provide free of cost boarding and lodging facilities to the old age women who are homeless and have no one to look after them.
We want a country free from homelessness, where everyone has a place to call home and can expect the support they need to keep it. Our vision outlines what needs to happen for homelessness to end. Our strategy describes the approach we will take to get there.
